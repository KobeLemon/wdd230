Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to create a center of commerce where businesses can join together to regulate each other and create a fair business society for both the businesses and the consumers. I want to make a space where citizens can see businesses that are verified as reputable and upstanding. I want to build a network that has events and fundraisers to bring the community together and highlights the hardworking business owners.
